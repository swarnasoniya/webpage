The GRI Standards are regularly reviewed to ensure they reflect global best practices for sustainability reporting, helping thousands of organizations around the world respond to emerging information demands from stakeholders and regulators.

SASB Standards enable business around the world to identify, manage and communicate financially-material sustainabilty topics on a company's financial condition or operating performance.

Visit GRI

CDP is a global disclosure system that enables companies, cities, states and regions to measure adn manage environmental impacts by focusing on Energy Consumption, and Water data.

Visit CDP

The TCFD recommendations are structured around 4 areas goverance, strategy, risk management, & metrics and targetrs with an aim to promote more informed investment, credit, and insurance underwriting decisions through better reporting of climate-related risks and opportunities.

Visit TCFD

SASB Standards enable business around the world to identify, manage and communicate financially-material sustainabilty topics on a company's financial condition or operating performance.

Visit SASB

Starting FY 2022-23, the top 1,000 listed companies by market capitalization are mandated to submit Business Responsibility and Sustainability Reports (BRSR). BRSR emphasizes measurable metrics for improved sector and time period comparability.

Visit BRSR ESG

The SGX ESG Framework promotes transparency and accountability, cultivating responsible business practices with global sustainability goals. It aids businesses in navigating both regional and global sustainability complexities while catering to the specific needs of the Singaporean market.

Visit SGX ESG
